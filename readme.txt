A community add-on for myCRED: the adaptive and powerful points management system for WordPress.

== Description ==

This add-on configures events, hooks and others to provide with essential community engagement features such as Social Networks, Surveys, Polls or Voting Systems.

**Increase community engagement tenfold – Rewarding your users for community engagement.**

= Features =

**- Social Network Events:** Automatically award or deduct points from your user’s balance for their interaction on social networks.
**- Survey/Polls Events:** Automatically award or deduct points from your user’s balance for their interaction on surveys and/or public/private polls.
**- Voting system Events:** Automatically award or deduct points from your user’s balance for their interaction on a voting system.


= Documentation =

You can find extensive [documentation](http://github.com/UsingBlockchain/mycred-addon-community) on the myCred Community add-on on our Github.
